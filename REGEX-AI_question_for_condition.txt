Using REGEX
From: "<strong>Effect</strong> 1d3 Dex damage, 1 Con damage, and nauseated for 1 round"
Extract: "nauseated for 1 round"
where "nauseated" can be any word provided
where "for" may or may not be present
where 1 can be any number OR a die expression like 1d6
where "round" can be any of {minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w}

To extract the specific condition and duration from your text, use this regular expression: 

(\w+)\s+(?:for\s+)?(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s+(minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w)

How it works

(\w+): Captures the condition (e.g., "nauseated").
\s+(?:for\s+)?: Matches the space and makes "for" optional.
(\d+d\d+|\d+): Captures the value. It looks for a die expression (1d6) first, or a simple number (1) if no die is found.
\s+: Matches the space before the time unit.
(minutes|rounds|...): Captures the specific time unit from your provided list.

Capture Groups
Group 1: The condition name.
Group 2: The amount/dice.
Group 3: The time unit.
