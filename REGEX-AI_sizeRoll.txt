Using REGEX
From "sizeRoll(1,10,4,4)[Lightning] + 5[Strength]"
Return "sizeRoll(1,10,4,4)[Lightning]" and "(1,10,4,4)" and "Lightning"
where "sizeRoll" is always present
where "(1,10,4,4)" all data between the curved brackets are wanted
where "[Lightning]" may or may not be present and can be any word within the square brackets and is only after "sizeRoll(1,10,4,4)"

REGEX = /(sizeRoll\(([^)]+)\)(?:\[([^\]]+)\])?)/i;
