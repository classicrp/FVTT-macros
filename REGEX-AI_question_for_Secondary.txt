SECONDARY
=========
Using REGEX
From: '<strong>Initial Effect</strong> 1 Con; <strong>Secondary Effect</strong> unconscious 1d3 hours'
Extact: '<strong>Secondary Effect</strong> unconscious 1d3 hours' AND "unconscious" AND "1d3" AND "hours"
where "Effect" may or may not be present
where "unconcious" can be many words OR nothing
where 1d3 can be any dice expression OR a number
where "hours" can be any of {minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w} OR {Str|Dex|Con|Int|Wis|Cha}
where "damage" may or may not appear after "hours"


First Iteration
===============
To extract the Secondary Effect segment and its specific components (the condition, value, and unit), you can use the following regex pattern:

<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>\s*((.*?)\s+(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s+(minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w))

⚡ The Breakdown

<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>: Matches the header; the (?: Effect)? makes the word "Effect" optional.

\s*: Handles any spacing after the tag.

((.*?)\s+(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s+(...)) : This is the outer capture group for the full string.

(.*?): Capture Group 2 – The condition/description (e.g., "unconscious").

(\d+d\d+|\d+): Capture Group 3 – The dice roll (1d3) or a flat number (10).

(minutes|...|w): Capture Group 4 – The time unit based on your specific list.

🔍 Results for your example
Using the string: '<strong>Secondary Effect</strong> unconscious 1d3 hours'
Group 1: unconscious 1d3 hours
Group 2: unconscious
Group 3: 1d3
Group 4: hours

💡 Tip: Ensure your regex engine is set to case-insensitive if the input text varies (e.g., "Hours" vs "hours").

REGEX = /<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>\s*((.*?)\s+(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s+(minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w))/i

Second Iteration (extra where clauses)
================
To extract the secondary effect and its components, use this regular expression:

/(<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>\s*(([\w\s]*?)\s*(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s*(minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w|Str|Dex|Con|Int|Wis|Cha)(?:\s*damage)?))/i

🧬 Capture Group Breakdown
Group 1: The full string (<strong>Secondary Effect</strong> unconscious 1d3 hours)
Group 2: The effect description, value, and unit (unconscious 1d3 hours)
Group 3: The descriptive words/condition (unconscious)
Group 4: The dice or number (1d3)
Group 5: The unit or ability score (hours)

🔍 Key Logic Used
<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>: Matches the header; "Effect" is optional.
([\w\s]*?): Non-greedily captures any words (the condition) before the numbers start.
(\d+d\d+|\d+): Matches standard dice notation (e.g., 1d20) or a flat integer.
(minutes|...|Cha): Matches your specific list of time units and ability scores.
(?:\s*damage)?: Checks for the word "damage" at the end but doesn't capture it.

REGEX = /(<strong>Secondary(?: Effect)?<\/strong>\s*(([\w\s]*?)\s*(\d+d\d+|\d+)\s*(minutes|rounds|minute|round|turns|hours|weeks|rnds|mins|turn|trns|hour|days|week|rnd|min|trn|hrs|day|wks|hr|wk|r|m|t|h|d|w|Str|Dex|Con|Int|Wis|Cha)(?:\s*damage)?))/i
