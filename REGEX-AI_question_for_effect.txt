Using REGEX
From: "<strong>Effect</strong> 1d3 Dex damage, 1 Con damage, and nauseated for 1 round"
Extract: "1d3 Dex damage" and "1 Con damage"
where "Dex" or "Con" can be any word
where 1 can be any number OR a die expression like 1d3

To extract the specific damage types and amounts from your string, use this pattern:

/(\d+(?:d\d+)?\s+\w+\s+damage)/gi

How it works

\d+ matches the initial number (e.g., 1).

(?:d\d+)? optionally matches a die expression (e.g., d3).

\s+\w+ matches the space and the damage attribute (e.g., Dex).

\s+damage matches the literal word "damage".
